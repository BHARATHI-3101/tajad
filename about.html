About Taj Hotels

Founded in 1903 by Jamsetji Tata, Taj Hotels is a timeless symbol of Indian luxury and hospitality. The iconic Taj Mahal Palace in Mumbai was the first property, envisioned not merely as a hotel, but as a beacon of excellence and cultural pride.

Today, Taj Hotels spans continents, offering guests a blend of regal heritage, modern elegance, and heartfelt service. From palatial retreats to urban sanctuaries, every Taj property tells a story of tradition, innovation, and unforgettable experiences. Read more
